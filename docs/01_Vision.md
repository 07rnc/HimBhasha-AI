# HimBhasha AI – Vision Document

## Vision Statement

To build the leading AI platform for Himachal Pradesh's regional languages, enabling every speaker to access technology, information, and digital services in their native language while preserving linguistic heritage for future generations.

---

# Mission Statement

HimBhasha AI aims to bridge the gap between modern Artificial Intelligence and Himachal Pradesh's regional languages by developing an intelligent multilingual assistant that understands, translates, explains, and communicates naturally.

Our first milestone is to support **Kangdi**, creating a strong technical and linguistic foundation that can later expand to **Mandeali, Kullui, Chambeali, Sirmauri, Kinnauri, and Bhoti**.

---

# Why We Exist

Artificial Intelligence is transforming how people learn, work, and communicate. However, much of this transformation is concentrated around English and a limited number of widely spoken languages.

Many regional languages of Himachal Pradesh remain underrepresented in AI systems, creating barriers for native speakers when accessing digital information, educational resources, and government services.

HimBhasha AI exists to reduce these barriers by making AI more accessible in the languages people use every day.

---

# The Problem We Want to Solve

Current AI systems offer broad multilingual capabilities, but support for Himachal's regional languages remains limited. This creates several challenges:

* Difficulty interacting with AI in native languages.
* Limited availability of educational resources.
* Challenges understanding official documents.
* Lack of digital tools that preserve regional linguistic heritage.
* Limited datasets for developing language technologies.

---

# Why Kangdi First?

We chose Kangdi as the MVP because:

* It is one of the most widely spoken regional languages in Himachal Pradesh.
* It represents a meaningful low-resource language challenge.
* Building a focused MVP allows us to validate the approach before expanding.
* A modular architecture enables future support for additional Himachali languages.

---

# Product Vision

HimBhasha AI is not intended to be another translation application.

Instead, it is envisioned as an AI platform capable of:

* Understanding speech
* Translating languages
* Explaining documents
* Answering user questions
* Supporting education
* Improving digital accessibility
* Preserving regional languages

Translation is one capability within a broader AI assistant.

---

# Core Principles

## Accessibility

Technology should communicate in the language users are most comfortable with.

---

## Cultural Preservation

Regional languages represent history, traditions, and identity. AI should help preserve—not replace—them.

---

## Human-Centered AI

Technology should simplify complex information and support users rather than overwhelm them.

---

## Trust

Responses should be transparent, understandable, and grounded in reliable information.

---

## Scalability

The platform should be designed to support additional languages without major architectural changes.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Over the next decade, HimBhasha AI aims to become a comprehensive AI platform for Himachal Pradesh's regional languages by expanding language coverage, improving speech technologies, supporting education, and contributing open datasets that encourage further research and innovation.

The long-term vision extends beyond one language or one application. It is to create an ecosystem where low-resource languages can participate meaningfully in the future of Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Measuring Success

Success will not be measured only by downloads or user numbers.

Instead, success means:

* Increasing access to AI for regional language speakers.
* Building high-quality multilingual datasets.
* Supporting educational and cultural initiatives.
* Encouraging community participation.
* Creating technology that is practical, inclusive, and sustainable.

---

# Closing Statement

Language is more than a means of communication—it carries culture, history, and identity.

HimBhasha AI seeks to ensure that as Artificial Intelligence becomes part of everyday life, the regional languages of Himachal Pradesh are not left behind, but become active participants in the next generation of digital innovation.
