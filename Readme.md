## 🌟 Overview

HimBhasha AI is an AI-powered multilingual assistant designed to improve digital accessibility for speakers of Himachal Pradesh's regional languages.

The project combines modern AI technologies—including speech recognition, OCR, multilingual translation, and conversational AI—to help users interact with information in their native language.

The MVP focuses on **Kangdi**, with a scalable architecture for supporting additional Himachali languages in future releases.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Many AI platforms primarily support English, Hindi, and other widely spoken languages. Regional languages of Himachal Pradesh remain underrepresented in modern AI systems.

This creates challenges in:

* Accessing digital services
* Understanding official documents
* Learning through AI
* Voice interaction
* Language preservation

HimBhasha AI aims to bridge this gap.
